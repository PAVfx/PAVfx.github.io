Servo Motors

High-Torque MG996R Digital Servo Motor features all metal gearing, resulting in 10kg torque and 55g weight! Thats a lot of power for this little servo. The MG996R is essentially an upgraded version of the famous MG995 servo, and features upgraded shock-proofing and a redesigned PCB and IC control system that make it much more accurate than its predecessor. The gearing and motor have also been upgraded to improve dead bandwith and centering.
